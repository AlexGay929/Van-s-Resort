VILLA-1

VILLA-1

Our Villa-1 room have high-end furnishings and decor, with attention to detail and a focus on comfort and luxury. This include plush bedding, high-quality linens, and high-end toiletries. You can also expect modern amenities like air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and Wi-Fi. The layout of our villa room includes a bedroom with a king-size or queen-size bed, a separate living area with comfortable seating, and a dining area with a table and chairs. Our room also feature a private terrace or balcony, often with views of the surrounding landscape. In terms of amenities, our villa room includes a private plunge pool, jacuzzi, and hot tub. We also offer butler service, private chefs, or other personalized services to enhance your stay. Overall, our villa room is a luxurious and spacious accommodation option that offers plenty of space to relax and unwind in a beautiful setting.

SPECTACULAR SUITE

SPECTACULAR SUITE

Our Suite Room is a luxurious accommodation option that offers ample space and a range of amenities to make your stay comfortable and enjoyable. Our suite rooms are larger and more luxurious than standard hotel rooms and are designed to provide guests with a high level of comfort and luxury. Our suite room includes a separate living area with comfortable seating, a flat-screen TV, and a minibar or refrigerator. The bedroom may feature a king or queen-size bed with plush bedding and high-quality linens. In addition, the suite have a private balcony or terrace with outdoor seating, offering stunning views of the resort's surroundings. Our Suite rooms have larger bathrooms than standard hotel rooms, with features such as a bathtub, a separate shower, and luxury toiletries. We also have additional half-baths or powder rooms for added convenience. In addition to these basic features, our suite rooms also offer a variety of additional amenities. It includes access to private lounges or beach clubs, personalized butler service, private pools, and jacuzzis or hot tubs.

ACTIVITIES

SCUBA DIVING

SCUBA DIVING

If the thought of disappearing beneath the coral islands excites you, Van's Resort has got to be your dream destination! Surrounded by the seas on all sides, the enchanting atolls of Philippines are the perfect place for adventurers to lose sight of land and levitate their souls as they take a plunge into the deep Philippine Ocean. Explore the miraculous world of coral reefs as you splash into the azure waters; Philippines is one of the best diving and snorkelling destinations in the world. The coral reefs are nothing short of paradise, to say the least, as they open up to you a whole new aquatic realm which is unlike anything else you’ve ever witnessed. Dive deep, and explore the mystic shades of the marine realm.

WATER SPORTS

WATER SPORTS

Being one of the most sought after beach destinations in the world, Van's Resort offers plenty of water sports opportunities. You can indulge in activities like canoeing, paddleboarding, knee boarding, kite surfing, wake boarding, wind surfing, water skiing, parasailing and banana boat rides. Set yourself free into the ocean for some water sports and witness the adventurous aspect of Van's Resort.
